Types of cloud computing:

SaaS- Software as service: A product that is run and managed by the service provider. eg: salesforce, gmail, office365.
PaaS- Platform as service: Focus on the deployment and management of your apps. eg: Elastic bean stalk, heroku.
IaaS- Infrastructure as service: The basic building blocks for cloud IT. Provides access to networking features, computers, and datastorage space. eg: Azure, AWS, GCP.
