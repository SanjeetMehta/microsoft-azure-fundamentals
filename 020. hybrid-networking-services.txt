Azure Front Door:
    Scalable and secure entry point for fast deliver of your global applications.

Azure Express Route:
    A connection b/w your on-premise to Azure cloud from 50Mbps to 10Gbps.

Virtual WAN:
    A networking service that brings many networking, security, and routing functionalities together to provide a single operational interface.

Azure Connection:
    A VPN connection securely connects two Azure local network via (IPsec).

Virtual NetworkGateway:
    A site-to-site VPN connection b/w an Azure virtual network and your local network.
